About Us

At Adam's Animals, we are passionate about helping pets live happy, healthy lives. Led by Adam Andewiel, a Certified Dog Trainer and Animal Behavior College graduate, we offer expert care and guidance for your furry family members.

With years of hands-on experience and a commitment to positive reinforcement, we provide a safe, encouraging environment where pets can thrive. Our services include certified dog training, group sessions, private lessons, virtual Zoom sessions, dog and cat behavioral assessments, dog walking, and pet sitting. Whether you need help shaping good habits, addressing behavioral challenges, or simply ensuring your pet is well cared for, we're here to support you every step of the way.
